ABOUT US

It all started as an after-school business for a group of university mates to earn some money for their expenses. With a hand-drawn logo, advertisement in a local paper, and advertising fund stashed in a ceramic dish, the endeavor was never expected to be more than a local moving company.

Now, more than 30 years later, THE MOVERS® has grown both domestically and internationally with more than 380 locations worldwide. In 2018, THE MOVERS® will operate in 43 cities with additional growth expected in the Pakistan, Afghanistan, Iran, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, India and China.

We’re proud of the more than 95 consecutive months of growth, the completion of nearly seven million moves and counting. THE MOVERS is poised for long-term record success!

THE MOVERS is the fastest-growing franchised moving company in the country and offers comprehensive home and business relocation and packing services. Our goal is to exceed customers expectations by customizing our moving services to specific needs. We move apartments, condominiums, homes, businesses, and everything in between – from very small to very large. Whether moving down the street, across the country, or to a new home or business building, we’re here to help with managing relocation stress.
