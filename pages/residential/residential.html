RESIDENTIAL

Whether you are relocating locally, within the province, across Canada, to/from the United States or overseas to anywhere on the Globe, The Movers is Always Ready, Always Reliable and more than happy to be of service to you. Our affiliations with United Van Lines/Mayflower Canada and IAM (International Association of Movers), provide us with a Global network of like-minded agents to provide a quality door-to-door relocation no matter where that door will be.

We understand that relocations can be stressful at the best of times. We can help minimize that stress with our fully experienced, hard-working crews. We have the right people and equipment in place to facilitate your move as carefully and efficiently as possible.

WE CAN HELP YOU WITH
  • The Movers can provide you with a stress-free Local Move from your current residence to your new home. We offer full moving services including packing of all cartons, partial packing of breakables only, or just moving of your furniture. You choose your moving requirements.
  • Following an “In Home” survey, we can provide you with an estimate specifically designed for your Moving Requirements. We will provide you with Wardrobe cartons on Move Day for your hanging clothes, covers for your upholstered furniture as well as mattress covers to protect your beds.
  • As a member of Mayflower Van Lines, The Movers’ residential Long Distance Moving division is a full service residential moving provider for shipments within Canada or to/from the United States. Whether you need complete packing of all cartons, partial packing of breakables, or just helpful information of packing cartons yourself, The Movers is more than happy to help. Once we complete our In Home Survey, and view the items you plan to ship, we will provide you with a quotation specifically designed for you moving requirements.
